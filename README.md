## English

### Overview

The M-VQA Challenge is a grand challenge for ACM Multimedia 2026, focusing on machine-oriented visual media quality assessment. This challenge aims to advance reliable image understanding for machine preference and support robust Embodied AI applications.

### Competition Structure

The competition is organized into three distinct tracks:

1. **Track 1: Absolute Performance Prediction (In-Silico)**
   - Predict the absolute accuracy of VLM models on images with controlled noise
   - Dataset: 12,400 images with 30 distortion types

2. **Track 2: Relative Degradation Assessment (In-Silico)**
   - Predict relative performance drop compared to baseline on clean images
   - Focus on sensitivity to increasing levels of image degradation

3. **Track 3: Real-World Robot Deployment (Real-Robot)**
   - Evaluation on physical robot platforms with 100 robotic arms
   - Only top 10 teams from Tracks 1 and 2 are eligible

## 中文

### 概述

M-VQA 挑战赛是 ACM Multimedia 2026 的一个重大挑战，专注于机器导向的视觉媒体质量评估。该挑战赛旨在推进机器偏好的可靠图像理解，支持稳健的具身 AI 应用。

### 比赛结构

比赛分为三个不同的赛道：

1. **赛道 1：绝对性能预测（仿真）**
   - 预测 VLM 模型在含噪声图像上的绝对准确率
   - 数据集：12,400 张图像，30 种失真类型

2. **赛道 2：相对性能下降评估（仿真）**
   - 预测与原始干净图像相比的相对性能下降
   - 关注对图像退化程度增加的敏感性

3. **赛道 3：真实世界机器人部署（真机）**
   - 在配备 100 个机械臂的物理机器人平台上进行评估
   - 仅赛道 1 和 2 的前 10 支队伍有资格参加
